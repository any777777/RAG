o Orbit Control System (Station Keeping):
• It is important that a GEO satellite be kept in its 
correct orbital slot.
• The equatorial ellipticity of the Earth causes GEO 
satellites to drift slowly along the orbit at East 
and West.
• To correct this drift, an oppositely directed 
velocity component is imparted by means of 
jets, which are pulsed once every 2 or 3 weeks.
• These maneuvers are termed East-West Station-
Keeping Maneuvers.
