 Insights on Frequency Selection:
•  The frequencies selected for satellite comm. are 
determined by 3 factors: 
a) The absorption of the atmosphere as a function of 
frequency. 
b) The antenna size needed to produce a beam with 
the required angular spread. 
c) International agreements/regulations. 
Absorption of the Atmosphere: The next graph 
shows the resonant absorption peaks due to 
different molecules in the atmosphere at particular 
frequencies.
