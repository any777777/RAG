Q3. [5 Points] LEO Satellite Coverage
A LEO satellite at an altitude of 1000 km provides service to a circular region with a
radius of 200 km around its sub-satellite point in the Ka band (23 GHz). Assuming a
satellite antenna aperture efficiency of 0.75, find the following:
1) [2 Points] 3-dB beam width of the antenna that will provide this coverage.
2) [1.5 Points] Radius of the satellite antenna.
3) [1.5 Points] Gain of the satellite antenna.
Q4. [3 Points] Receiver Performance
Consider the receive side of an earth station with equipment ambient temperature T0
= 290 K, antenna noise temperature TA = 60 K, waveguide loss L1 = 0.3 dB, LNA
equivalent noise temperature Te2 = 150 K and gain G2 = 60 dB, mixer equivalent noise
temperature Te3 = 11 × 103 K, and antenna gain GA = 65.53 dB. Find the system noise
temperature referred to the antenna feed, and then compute the antenna gain-to-
noise-temperature ratio G/T.
