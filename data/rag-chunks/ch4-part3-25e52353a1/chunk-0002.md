Example: 
• The satellite used  in  this example (see Tables 4.4a 
and 4.4b)  is  in geostationary earth orbit and carries 
24 C-band transponders, each with a bandwidth of 36 
MHz. The downlink band is 3.7-4.2GHz and the 
satellite uses orthogonal circular polarizations to 
provide an effective RF bandwidth of 864 MHz. The 
satellite provides coverage of  the visible earth, which  
subtends an  angle  of approximately  17°  from a  
satellite  in  a  geostationary  orbit, by  using  a global 
beam antenna.

together[3-  dB  beamwidth = ඥ33000/𝐺𝐺 where G  is
