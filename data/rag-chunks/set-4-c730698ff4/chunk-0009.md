• Frequency Demultiplexing – divides input 
signal into sub-bands to reduce non-linear 
distortion during amplification.  
• Each frequency sub-band is processed by a 
single transponder. 
• Equalizers – Correct for phase differences 
between the different frequency components 
of a signal, introduced by filters, de-
multiplexers etc.

• HPAs – High Power Amplifiers – to increase 
power levels before re-transmission on the 
down-link.  HPAs have non-linear behavior 
(should be avoided). 
• Bandpass Filters at various points remove out-
of-band products from the HPAs and reduce the 
background noise, but they cannot remove in-
band products .
