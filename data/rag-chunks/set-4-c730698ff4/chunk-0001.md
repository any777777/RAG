Introduction
• A communication Satellite is designed to operate 
properly 10 to 15 years to have enough revenues.
• The main components of a communication satellite 
system can be divided as:
Space Segments: Consist of the satellite & ground 
facilities providing the Tracking, Telemetry 
Command & Monitoring (TTC&M) functions and 
logistics support.
Earth Segments: The part of a communication-
satellite system, constituted by the Earth Stations 
used for transmitting and receiving the traffic 
signals of all kinds to and from the satellites
