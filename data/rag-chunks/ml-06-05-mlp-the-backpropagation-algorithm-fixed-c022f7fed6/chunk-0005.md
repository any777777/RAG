Weaknesses of the BP Algorithm
• The convergence obtained from BP learning is 
very slow.
• BP convergence is not guaranteed as the result 
may generally converge to any local minimum on 
the error surface, since stochastic gradient 
descent exists on a surface which is not flat.
• BP learning requires preprocessing prior to 
training (typically input scaling or normalization).
