Course Learning Outcomes (CLOs) 
Upon successful completion of this course, the participant will be able to: 
CLO1: Identify the basic concepts of satellite communications: Orbits, orbital mechanics and 
parameters, launching aspects, telemetry, tracking and command system (e). 
CLO2:  Evaluate look angles (elevation and azimuth) and satellite visibility to Earth Station (e). 
CLO3:  Understand the radio propagation channel, propagation delay effects, satellite antennas 
and patterns suitable to the two-way Satellite-Earth station links (h). 
CLO4:  Analyze and design link budget, uplink/downlink, C/N ratio, losses and noise sources, 
G/T ratio for earth stations (e). 
CLO5:  Understand Transponders: Carrier and transponder capacity, Single carrier and multi- 
              carrier transponders (h). 
CLO6:  Understand the various satellite multiplexing and multiple access techniques, Direct Digital TV 
Broadcast systems (i).  
CLO7:  Understand the fundamentals of color TV transmission and reception (i). 
CLO8:  Identify modern satellite systems, applications and tools such as GPS and MSAT, Network 
 topologies and VSATs (k).
