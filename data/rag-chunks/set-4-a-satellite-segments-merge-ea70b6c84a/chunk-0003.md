 Spin Stabilization: It may be achieved with 
cylindrical satellites. The satellite is constructed 
so that it is mechanically balanced about one 
particular axis, then is set spinning around it. 
• For GEO sats, the spin axis is adjusted to be 
parallel to the N-S axis of the Earth, as shown 
next. 
• Impulse-type thrusters, or jets, can be used to 
increase the spin rate again and to shift the axis 
back to its correct N-S orientation.
