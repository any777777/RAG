Reflector Antennas
Reflector antennas provide:
• High gain
• Narrow beamwidths
They are illuminated by one or more feed horns. The most common reflector shape
is the paraboloid.
Placing the feed at the focal point ensures:
• Equal path lengths
• Uniform phase across the aperture
• Maximum gain
Modified reflector shapes are often used to tailor the beam to a specific coverage
region.
