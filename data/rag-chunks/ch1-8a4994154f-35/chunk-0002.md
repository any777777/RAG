## Slide 13

Cellular Generations

Cellular is the fastest growing sector of communication industry (exponential growth )
First Generation (1G - ex. NMT, ca. 1982 - ): Analog 25 or 30 KHz FM, voice only, mostly vehicular communications.
Second Generation (2G – GSM, ADC, JDC and IS 95 ): Narrowband TDMA and CDMA, voice and low bit-rate data, portable units.

2.5G -: Enhancements to 2G network for increased data transmission capabilities (HSCSD, GPRS,  EDGE and IS 95B).

HSCSD: High Speed Circuit Data:  
	( 4 time slot x 14.4 kbps=57.6 kbps )

GPRS: General Packet Radio Services: 
	( 8 time slot x 21.4 kbps= 171.2 kbps)

EDGE : Enhanced Data for GSM Evolution
	 (8psk): 547.2 kbps
