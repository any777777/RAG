1. Project Overview
This course project aims to explore recent advancements in modern satellite communi-
cation systems, focusing on emerging technologies such as LEO mega-constellations, 6G
integration, AI-driven satellite networks, and optical space communications.
Students will work in groups to investigate a selected topic, develop a comprehensive
technical report, and present their findings professionally.
2. Group Formation
• Each group must consist of 4 students.
• Each group selects one topic only.
• No duplication of topics is allowed across groups.
3. Proposed Project Topics (Emerging Technologies
in Satellite Communications)
1. Integration of Satellite Networks in 6G (NTN – Non-Terrestrial Net-
works)
• Role of LEO/MEO/GEO in 6G architectures
• Seamless space–air–ground network integration
• Challenges: latency, mobility management, and standardization
2. Artificial Intelligence for Autonomous Satellite Operations
• AI-based resource allocation and routing
• Fault detection and self-healing satellite systems
• Impact on network efficiency and reliability
3. LEO Mega-Constellations and Global Internet Coverage
• Systems such as Starlink, OneWeb, and Kuiper
• Spectrum sharing and interference management
• Integration with terrestrial networks
4. Reconfigurable Intelligent Surfaces (RIS) in Satellite Communications
• Smart signal reflection and propagation control
• Coverage enhancement in urban and shadowed areas
• Integration challenges with satellite links
5. Quantum Satellite Communications and Cybersecurity
